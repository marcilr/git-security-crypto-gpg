GnuPG - The GNU Privacy Guard
=============================
GnuPG is a complete and free implementation of the OpenPGP standard as
defined by RFC4880[1] (also known as PGP).  GnuPG allows to encrypt and
sign your data and communication, features a versatile key management
system as well as access modules for all kinds of public key directories.
GnuPG, also known as GPG, is a command line tool with features for easy
integration with other applications.  A wealth of frontend applications[2] 
and libraries are available.  Version 2 of GnuPG also provides support for
S/MIME and Secure Shell (ssh).
